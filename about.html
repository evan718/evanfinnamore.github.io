About Me

"The greatest glory in living lies not in never falling, but in rising every time we fall."
– Nelson Mandela

Welcome to a space dedicated to understanding, healing, and growth. My journey in the field of mental health is deeply intertwined with personal experiences and a passion for helping others. With an undergraduate degree in Kinesiology and a graduate degree in Rehabilitation Sciences from the University of British Columbia, I have explored the profound connections between physical well-being and mental health. Now, as I embark on the final year of my Master's in Counselling Psychology, I am excited to become a registered clinical counsellor.

Having faced my own challenges with mental health, I understand the courage it takes to seek help and the strength required to pursue change. These experiences have shaped my empathetic approach and commitment to providing a supportive environment where you feel heard and understood.

"The curious paradox is that when I accept myself just as I am, then I can change."
– Carl Rogers

My goal is to empower you with the tools and insights to better understand yourself and navigate your journey toward well-being. Together, we can transform obstacles into opportunities for personal growth.

Change is not only possible but within your reach. As Viktor E. Frankl wisely said, "When we are no longer able to change a situation, we are challenged to change ourselves." I am here to support you in embracing this challenge, fostering hope, and building a future where you feel confident in your ability to thrive.

I invite you to explore the resources available and join a community committed to healing and positive change. Let's embark on this journey together toward a hopeful and fulfilling future.
