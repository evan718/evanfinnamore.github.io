Think of Your Brain as a Control Center

Imagine your brain as a sophisticated control center with three key areas working together:

  • The Commander (Prefrontal Cortex): Makes decisions and plans
  • The Alert System (Amygdala): Handles emotions and warnings
  • The Memory Keeper (Hippocampus): Stores experiences and memories
